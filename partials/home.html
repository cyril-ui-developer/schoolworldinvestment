Welcome to School World Investment Website

School World Investment Limited started in 2005 inspired by Nkem Raji and Grace Achike whose love of travel expanded to the realisation that most kids end up spending a three months vacation just shopping, watching Tv and going to Macdonald's or KFC; when kids were asked to describe their vacation, they couldn't write about the historical key landmarks. The museums were never a go area in the holiday plan. We decided, knowing that travelling was an adventure waiting to be explored and each child being part of this world of experience deserves to have a meaningful educative tour while having fun at the same time.

Lastly, it is not just taking the sound, site and curriculum from their classroom and bring the subject to life that matter only, it is the bond of friendship they make by learning to create and set boundaries that will mould that creativity and dream to be the very best of who they want to be.

We look forward to having you go on tour with us.

Leadership Forum
