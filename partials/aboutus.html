About Us

We understand that providing educational tours for children from ages 4 plus to 20 years is a huge responsibility and safety is our keyword to ensure that our parents, guardians are relaxed knowing that their children/wards are in safe hands. Our management team are always available to guide your children/wards every step of the way and our partners in the camp programme are guided by the governing bodies for camp programme in that country.

We create memorable experience in a child’s life by making our tour content, exploration of the country we visit by taking their language(s), cultures, traditions, innovations and beautiful famous landmarks a reality that always stays in a child’s mind for a lifetime.

With our partners in the camping programme. Our children have conquered their fears & uncertainities through their participation in initiative programmes, adventure activities ( orienteering, exploration and team challenge) by showing their talents, building developmental life skills(respect, tolerance, responsibilities) and understanding and aprreciationg that life is wonderful adventure if you embrace it. To live it, you have to experience it.
